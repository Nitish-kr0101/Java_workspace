What is Cricket?

Cricket is a bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of eleven players. It is popular in many countries and has several formats ranging from Test matches (5 days) to One Day Internationals (50 overs per side) and T20 (20 overs per side).

This page lives under the application's /static resources and is served through the application controller at /cricket.

Key Rules (Short)

  • Two teams of 11 players.
  • One team bats, trying to score runs; the other bowls and fields to dismiss the batsmen and limit runs.
  • An innings ends when 10 batsmen are out, or overs are completed, or the batting team declares (in some formats).
  • Runs are scored by running between the wickets or by hitting boundaries (4 or 6).

Dismissals

  1. Bowled
  2. Caught
  3. LBW (Leg Before Wicket)
  4. Run out
  5. Stumped
